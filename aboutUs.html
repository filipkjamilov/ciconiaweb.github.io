About Us

A Quiet Stay Rooted in Nature and Simplicity

Ciconia Apartments was created with one clear vision: to offer a calm, comfortable, and sustainable place to stay in one of the most peaceful corners of Macedonia. Located in the charming village of Stojakovo, near the southern border of the country, our apartments provide a restful environment surrounded by nature, tradition, and a slower pace of life.

What's in a Name?

The name Ciconia comes from the Latin word for stork, a graceful bird known for its seasonal visits to our region. Stojakovo is home to many storks, and seeing them nesting on rooftops and telephone poles is a familiar and comforting sight here. In Latin, Ciconia is pronounced [ˈkik.o.ni.a], and in Italian, the word is also cicogna, reflecting the same root and meaning. We chose this name to honor the connection between our village and these elegant birds, which represent peace, renewal, and homecoming.

A New Space with Purpose

Our apartments are part of a newly built complex with all-new furnishings and modern comfort. Each space was designed to be non-smoking and as sustainable as possible, incorporating thoughtful choices in materials, energy use, and furnishings. We aim to provide not just a place to sleep—but a place to rest, recharge, and enjoy the simplicity of life in a quiet village.

Welcome to Stojakovo

Stojakovo is a small village in the Gevgelija region of North Macedonia, just a short distance from the southern border. It’s known for its peaceful setting, welcoming community, and its unique blend of nature and tradition.
